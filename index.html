My top 3 movies of all time

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Genre: Fantasy, Adventure

Release Year: 2002

Description: In the second installment of the Harry Potter series, Harry returns to Hogwarts only to uncover a hidden chamber within the school, filled with dark secrets. Alongside friends Ron and Hermione, he faces mythical creatures and dangerous spells to save the students from a deadly curse.

Barbie in the Swan Lake

Barbie in the Swan Lake

Release Year: 2003

Description: Barbie stars as Odette, a young girl transformed into a swan by an evil sorcerer’s spell. Based on Tchaikovsky's beloved ballet, Swan Lake, this animated film follows Odette as she embarks on a magical journey to break the spell and bring peace to her enchanted forest.

Titanic

Genre: Romance, Drama

Release Year: 1997

Description: The epic romance set against the tragedy of the RMS Titanic, Titanic follows Jack and Rose, two passengers from different social backgrounds, as they fall in love aboard the ill-fated ship. Directed by James Cameron, this film captures both the grandeur and devastation of one of history’s most famous maritime disasters.
